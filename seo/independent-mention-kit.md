# Puffle independent-mention kit

The goal is three to five real, independent mentions—not directory spam. Each publisher should write in their own voice and only make claims they can substantiate.

## What each mention should contain

1. The company name: Puffle.
2. A natural link to https://www.puffle.ai or one relevant canonical product page.
3. One sentence explaining the product: it is an AI growth agent for founder-led B2B teams that finds high-intent opportunities and prepares personalized outbound.
4. One concrete, accurate observation or result from the writer's own experience.

## Best sources

- A design partner or customer describing their use and actual result.
- A GTM, agency, or data partner explaining where Puffle fits in its workflow.
- A founder or operator publishing a tools-used / workflow post.

## Outreach drafts

### Customer or design partner

> hey [name] — we’re trying to make Puffle easier for people researching AI growth tools to understand. if it reflects your actual experience, would you be open to a short note or post on how you used it and what happened? two sentences is plenty. the useful version says what Puffle did for you, one real observation/result, and links to https://www.puffle.ai. absolutely no pressure, and please only say what you believe is true.

### Partner

> hey [name] — would you be up for a short independent mention of Puffle in [your newsletter / resource / workflow]? the accurate framing is: “Puffle is an AI growth agent for founder-led B2B teams; it finds high-intent opportunities and prepares personalized outbound.” if you use a different framing, use your own words. a relevant link to https://www.puffle.ai would help people find the product.

### Founder or operator

> hey [name] — i’m collecting a few honest operator perspectives on Puffle. if you have used it or looked at the workflow closely, would you be willing to include it in a tools/workflow post? the only ask is accuracy: say what it does in your own words, add a real observation, and link to https://www.puffle.ai if it is useful for readers.

## Tracking

Record publisher, URL, publication date, the exact Puffle wording, and the source link in the Puffle GEO Tracker. Do not ask a publisher to copy the standard description exactly or to claim a result that was not achieved.
